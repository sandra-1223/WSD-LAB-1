Employment Opportunities

Build Your Career with Baseline.
  1. Home
  2. Careers
In addition to meaningful, challenging and rewarding work, you’ll have the opportunity to give back to your community, make dreams come true (for our self-builders!) and you will find the support, training and courses that you will need to advance your career.

Why you should apply?

You want a career in a stable and thriving business.

We offer training and flexibility; supporting our employees for more than 30 years.

You use a computer for much of your working day.

That’s why we offer free regular eye tests to our employees who use a visual display unit.

You like to keep fit and stay active.

That’s why we offer a corporate gym membership at Halo Leisure.

You are planning for the future.

You will be put into a pension scheme through Automatic Enrolment.

You drive to work.

We offer free parking at our head office and factories.

You cycle to work.

We provide bike shelters at each of our locations on Rotherwas Industrial Estate.

You enjoy culture.

You will be eligible for a Courtyard staff discount card, receiving 10% discount on sales price at the Courtyard Centre for Arts, Hereford

And the longer you work with us, the better it gets…

You work 3-5 years. We gift you an extra day annual leave.

You work 5-7 years. That increases to an additional two days’ annual leave.

You work 7-10 years. You receive an extra three days’ annual leave.

Work with us for more than 10 years. Your annual leave allowance is increased by four days.

You will start on 21 days’ holiday per year plus bank holidays.

How to apply

This depends on the role. You can email/post a copy of your CV along with a covering letter or, if instructed, complete our application form in full, and return it to hr@baseline.co.uk or post to us marked for HR’s attention.
